Movement Stats

Base speed: 180 px/s
Slow mode: 0.78× (≈140 px/s)
Diagonal: normalized (÷ √2)
Healing move speed: 10 px/s

Dash

Dash speed: 450 px/s
Duration: 0.15s (invincible)
Cooldown: 1.5s
Must be moving to dash

Energy System

Max energy: 100 · Drain (moving): 18/s · Regen (slow mode): 14/s · Regen (idle): 20/s
At 0 energy → forced slow mode until 20% recovered. Gravity Boots disable energy drain entirely.

Combat

Base Shooting Stats

Base damage: 18
Shoot cooldown: 0.25s
Bullet speed: 500 px/s
Bullet range: 350 px
Bullet spread: 0.03 rad
Invincibility after hit: 0.2s

Ammo System

Each gun has a clip size and reload time. Ammo is consumed from your backpack — rarest ammo loads first.

Free fallback: White ammo (infinite, no bonus)
Ammo rarity affects damage multiplier and visual style.
Some ammo types have wall penetration or instant kill.

Damage Falloff

Within base range → full damage. Beyond that → damage falls off by 0.5×, linearly decreasing to 0 over the next range unit.

Kill Feedback

Kill Chain (kills within 3.5s):
1 → TARGET DOWN · 2 → DOUBLE ELIM · 3 → TRIPLE ELIM
4 → QUAD ELIM · 5+ → CHAIN x{N}
Kill Streak (cumulative in raid):
3+ → COMBAT STREAK · 5+ → PRESSURE
7+ → OVERWATCH · 10+ → DOMINATING

Shield System

Shields come from armor and helmets of Purple rarity and above. Higher-rarity shields are consumed first.

Shield Absorption Rules

Ammo vs Shield RarityEffect
Ammo lower than shieldShield absorbs 100% of damage (overflow goes to HP)
Ammo equal to shieldShield absorbs 30% of damage
Ammo higher than shieldBoth shield and HP take full damage
Instant Kill ammoBypasses shields entirely
Shields regenerate continuously at their shieldRegen rate (HP/s).

AI Operators

Human-like AI bots that roam the map with full loadouts. They come in three types: Searcher (loots crates), Runner (seeks extraction), and Fighter (hunts players). Spawn in squads of 3 (friendly fire disabled within squad).

Extraction

Extraction radius: 50 px · Extraction time: 3.0 seconds (stand still in zone)
Leaving the zone resets the timer. Extraction points are marked on the minimap.

Easy / Advanced: 4 extraction points near map edges (safe zone)
Hell: 2 extraction points near map edges
Chaos: 1 extraction point in center high-value zone — locked until half operators die

Map & Zones

Zone Layout (concentric rings from center)

High-Value (center) — distance < 25% from center. Best loot, most danger.
Combat (middle ring) — 25%–60% from center. Good loot, many enemies.
Safe (outer) — > 60% from center. Starting area, basic loot.

Zones are separated by walls with fixed entrances. Combat ring has N/S/E/W entrances. High-value ring has N/E/W entrances (Chaos: N only).

Map Features

Tile size: 48×48 px
Random rooms (4–10 × 4–8 tiles) with doors
Random wall clusters for cover
Connectivity guaranteed (flood-fill + corridors)
Health packs: 10 per area-scale unit (heal 30 HP)

Spawning

Player: safe zone near edge
Operators: safe zone only (min 170px apart)
Drones: combat + safe zones
Sentinels: combat + high-value zones
